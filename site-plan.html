Coach Mav's Personal Training - Site Plan

Site Name

Coach Mav's Personal Training

This name reflects a professional and personal training brand that is owned by Coach Mav. It communicates fitness expertise, personalization, and brand identity.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide potential clients with information about personal training services, showcase fitness certifications and skills, and allow clients to contact and connect with Coach Mav for consultations or bookings.

Scenarios

  • What training services does Coach Mav offer for muscle building or weight loss?
  • How can I view Coach Mav's fitness certifications and qualifications?

Color Schema

Black - Used for headers and body text.

White - Used for background color to provide contrast and cleanliness.

Blue (#008cba) - Used for buttons and accent areas.

Typography

Font Family: Roboto

  • Headings: Roboto Bold
  • Body Text: Roboto Regular
